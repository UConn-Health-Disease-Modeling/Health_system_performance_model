An ODE-based infectious disease model incorporating state-dependent, capacity-constrained service processes to study endogenous delays, bottlenecks, and nonlinear demand–capacity interactions in healthcare systems. The repository includes three independent Python notebooks:

Health_performance_model_dynamics.ipynb

Simulates the core model dynamics, illustrating how service demand, demand-dependent care-seeking rates, and capacity constraints interact to shape system behavior and key outputs, including cumulative treatment and the prevalence of care-seekers.

Health_performance_bar_plots.ipynb Provides a comparative analysis of intervention effects under different capacity scenarios (low, medium, and high), highlighting how system constraints modify the impact of improvements in the care cascade.

Health_performance_TB_Contour.ipynb Generates contour plots to examine the sensitivity of model outcomes—particularly cumulative treatment—to variations in capacity limits and patient delay parameters, offering insight into threshold effects and system performance across parameter space.
